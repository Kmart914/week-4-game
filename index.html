Crystals Collector!

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

By clicking on one of the four crystals below, you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You will win the game by matvhing your total score to the random number. You will lose the game if your score exceeds the random number.

The value of each crystal will be hidden until you click on the crystal.

Each time the game starts, the game will reset the random number as well as the value of each crystal.
